🟢 Beginner – Rebuild & Reset

For men and women 40+ who are starting fresh or returning after time off.

This isn’t about going hard—it’s about going smart. We focus on building foundational strength, restoring mobility, improving posture, and boosting confidence. Think of it as fitness rehab meets future-proofing.

  • ✔️ A custom 6-week program tailored to your pace and needs
  • ✔️ 5 targeted workouts per week
  • ✔️ A clean, simple menu to support your goals
  • ✔️ Guided meditation and relaxation tools
  • ✔️ Stretching sessions to improve flexibility and recovery

🔶 Intermediate – Stronger, Leaner, Sharper

For 40+ folks who’ve got some experience and want to step it up.

We use time-efficient workouts to increase lean muscle, strengthen your metabolism, and improve cardiovascular health—without crash diets or injury.

  • ✔️ A custom 6-week plan designed to push you safely and steadily
  • ✔️ 5 results-driven workouts per week
  • ✔️ A clean eating menu with portion and prep guidance
  • ✔️ Guided meditation and breathwork for focus and recovery
  • ✔️ Stretching protocols for mobility and injury prevention

🔴 Pro – Performance at Any Age

For high performers 40+ who want to train with intention and precision.

This program is for experienced lifters, weekend warriors, and everyday badasses. It’s tailored with age-smart progressions, recovery strategies, and metabolic firepower.

  • ✔️ A high-level 6-week custom program tailored to your goals
  • ✔️ 5 challenging, performance-based workouts per week
  • ✔️ A nutrient-dense meal plan to support muscle, focus, and recovery
  • ✔️ Guided meditation and mental reset tools
  • ✔️ Advanced mobility and stretching sessions
