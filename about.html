Yassir Nadifi is the founder of Nadifi FTNSS, a certified NASM trainer and nutrition coach with over a decade of experience helping clients transform their lives. After reversing his own type 2 diabetes, he built a concierge wellness business that’s helped clients collectively lose over 2,000 pounds.

He blends functional strength, corrective training, and low-carb nutrition with modern tools and old-school grit. Yassir specializes in programs for men and women over 40 who want to build strength, lose fat, and take back control of their energy, health, and focus.

All Nadifi FTNSS programs are self-paced and designed for you to complete on your own schedule. You'll receive downloadable plans that walk you through every step—no live training required. If you ever have questions, Yassir offers email support and a subscriber-only newsletter packed with tips, motivation, and coaching advice.

This approach is perfect for independent, motivated people who want expert guidance without the pressure of appointments or gym memberships. Whether you're rebuilding after a health scare or leveling up your performance, Nadifi FTNSS gives you the roadmap to succeed on your own terms.
